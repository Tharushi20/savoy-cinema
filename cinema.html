Cinemas

Cinema- C1

Step into the cutting-edge Cinema Hall C1, located conveniently at the main lobby upon entering Savoy. Hosting 135 seats, C1 boasts an unparalleled Dolby Atmos surround sound system alongside ultra-bright 4K projection with 3D capabilities. Designed with wheelchair accessibility in mind, each seat ensures a first-class audiovisual experience, setting the standard in cinematic immersion.

Cinema- C2

Discover Cinema Hall C2 on Level 3, accessible both from Savoy's main entrance and directly from the mall. With a seating capacity of 70 viewers, C2 features a state-of-the-art Dolby 7.1 surround sound system and superior 2K laser projection with 3D capabilities. Offering plush seating and unobstructed views, C2 is ideal for private screenings, corporate events, or memorable parties.
